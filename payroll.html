File format guide

Column names are case-insensitive and flexible — the app accepts common variations.

⏱️ Time clock CSV (Step 1 — required)

One row per punch. Missing clock-out is auto-flagged.

employee_id, clock_in,            clock_out
EMP001,      2025-06-02 08:02:00, 2025-06-02 17:05:00
EMP002,      2025-06-02 07:55:00, 2025-06-02 16:45:00
EMP003,      2025-06-02 09:00:00,                     ← flagged

Accepted formats: YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS · M/D/YYYY H:MM AM/PM

💵 Rates & PTO balances file (Step 2 — upload option)

The app accepts files with or without headers. If your file is just two columns (name and rate), it will auto-detect and import correctly. Add a salaried column with yes/no, or leave hourly_rate blank to mark someone as salaried/exempt.

employee_id, name,          hourly_rate, salaried, sick_bank, vacation_bank
EMP001,      Jane Smith,    22.50,       no,       40,        80
EMP002,      Carlos Reyes,  18.75,       no,       40,        80
EMP003,      Maria Torres,  ,            yes,      ,

Minimal two-column format (no headers needed — name in col 1, rate in col 2):

Jane Smith,   22.50
Carlos Reyes, 18.75
Maria Torres, salaried

🌴 Time off file (Step 3 — upload option)

type must be sick or vacation. requested_on is only required for vacation entries — leave blank for sick.

employee_id, date,       type,     hours, requested_on
EMP001,      2025-06-05, sick,     8,
EMP002,      2025-06-03, vacation, 8,     2025-05-15
EMP003,      2025-06-04, vacation, 4,     2025-06-03  ← <14 days → flagged

⚖️ Overtime rules

California (default): First 8h/day = regular · 8–12h = 1.5× · Over 12h = 2× · Weekly hours over 40 (beyond daily OT) = 1.5×

Federal: All hours over 40/week = 1.5×. No daily OT.

🚩 Flag reference

MISSED PUNCH Clock-in with no clock-out, or clock-out before clock-in.
LOW HOURS Worked + time off below expected for the period workdays.
SICK Sick time used — auto-excused, no notice required.
VACATION Vacation used with ≥14 days advance notice — excused.
VAC PENDING APPROVAL Vacation requested <14 days in advance — needs your sign-off.
HOLIDAY Company holiday in the period — 8h auto-added.
OT Employee has overtime hours.
