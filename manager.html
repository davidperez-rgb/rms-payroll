Columns are grouped and color-coded by category — click any header to sort. "Needs Your Review" = days flagged late with PTO on file that day — click Details to confirm whether it's excused or genuinely late. "Heads-Up" = a compliant but shorter lunch (30–52 min), tracked separately from actual violations, not counted against anyone.

No grace period on arrival — even 1 minute counts. If PTO is on file that day, review it yourself and mark it excused or confirmed. Meal break: under 30 min = violation (meal premium owed). 30–52 min = compliant heads-up, not a violation. over 67 min = long-lunch trend flag, not a violation. 53–67 min = normal.

* Sick payout = unused hrs × hourly rate (or annual salary ÷ 2080 if salaried). Vacation payout uses brackets based on monthly salary (annual ÷ 12, or hourly rate × ~173.3 standard monthly hrs) — $160/day up to $3,999/mo, $180 up to $4,999, $200 up to $6,999, $250 at $7,000+ — prorated by the hour (e.g. 7h in the $180 bracket pays 7/8 × $180).
